Who We Are?

Shree Krishna Cables , founded in 2007, is a leading manufacturer of high-quality wires and cables. With over a decade of industry experience, the company is certified with ISO 9001:2015 and holds ISI certifications for standards IS 694, IS 1293, and IS 9968 Part 1. These certifications reflect our commitment to quality and adherence to stringent industry regulations. At Shree Krishna Cables, we specialize in producing reliable and durable wire solutions that cater to a wide range of applications, ensuring customer satisfaction through innovation, precision, and performance.
