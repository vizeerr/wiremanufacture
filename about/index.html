About Us

Overview

Welcome to Dynamic Cables, one of the leading cable manufacturers in India, serving the energy and power sector both locally and abroad for over three decades! Situated in the heart of Rajasthan, our organization is inspired by a philosophy of product excellence, customer satisfaction, and technological advancement. At Dynamic Cables, we believe that modernization and compliance with the ever-changing requirements of the industry are crucial for success. To ensure this, we have three state-of-the-art and modern manufacturing and testing facilities located in Rajasthan. Our products are CE certified and type-tested by internationally recognized testing labs such as CPRI, ERDA, and Tag Corporation, guaranteeing their superior quality and reliability.

Mission

With an increasing overseas footprint and a new plant in offing, the company is targeting Rs. 1500-2000 Cr. topline in ten years with an objective to keep pace with changing technology, manufacturing process, quality controls, environment and safety requirements, timely delivery, after sales service, customer satisfaction and winning customer confidence and trust.
