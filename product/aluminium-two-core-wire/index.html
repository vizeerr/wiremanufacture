Aluminium Two Core Wire

Aluminium two-core wire is an electrical cable consisting of two conductors made from high-quality aluminum. Known for its lightweight nature and excellent conductivity, this type of wire is commonly used in power distribution and electrical installations where reduced weight is advantageous. Its two-core design allows for the transmission of both live and neutral current, making it ideal for various applications, including residential, commercial, and industrial use. Aluminium two-core wire offers an economical solution without compromising on performance and is resistant to corrosion, ensuring longevity.

Product Specifications

  • Lightweight and easy to handle
  • Good electrical conductivity
  • Two insulated conductors
  • Corrosion resistant
  • Cost-effective alternative to copper
  • Suitable for various applications

Product Description

Aluminium two-core wire is designed with two insulated aluminum conductors, making it an ideal choice for electrical installations that require the transmission of both live and neutral currents. This wire type is particularly favored for its lightweight characteristics, which facilitate easier handling and installation, especially in overhead wiring and long-distance power transmission. Aluminium is a highly conductive material, allowing for effective electricity flow while remaining a cost-effective option compared to copper wires. The two-core configuration enhances its versatility, enabling use in various applications, from domestic wiring to larger-scale industrial setups. Additionally, aluminium two-core wires are resistant to corrosion, making them suitable for outdoor and humid environments. The insulation around each core provides safety by preventing short circuits and electrical faults. Available in various sizes and configurations, these wires can meet different current capacity requirements, ensuring efficient power distribution in residential, commercial, and industrial contexts. The combination of lightweight construction, good conductivity, and resistance to environmental factors makes aluminium two-core wire an excellent choice for those seeking reliable and economical wiring solutions. Overall, this wire type serves as a practical and efficient option for various electrical applications.
